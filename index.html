Privacy Policy for SyncTwo

Last updated: July 15, 2026

This privacy policy explains how SyncTwo ("the App"), developed by May Works, handles information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

SyncTwo does not require account creation or login, and does not directly collect any personally identifiable information from you.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data — including advertising identifiers and usage data — to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, in accordance with Google's own privacy policy. You can learn more about how Google uses this data here:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

On iOS, you will be asked for App Tracking Transparency permission before any tracking-related data is used for advertising purposes.

Data Storage

Game progress and settings (such as selected skins and daily challenge completion) are stored locally on your device only. This data is not transmitted to or stored on any server operated by May Works.

Children's Privacy

SyncTwo is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
